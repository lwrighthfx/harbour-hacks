About

HarbourHacks was started by Laura Wright, a grade 12 student at Citadel High School. After being a Future Tech Intern at Nokia Canada over the summer she realized for many other Canadian high school students hackathons were a regular weekend activity. However, in Halifax there are no in-person hackathons for high school students. In realization of this she created her own. Garnering support from SuperNOVA @ Dalhousie Univeristy for resources. Laura secured funding from the GLOCAL Foundnation in support of resources required.
